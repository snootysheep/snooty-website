In order to ensure the quality of our Services, we may collect data and information of your usage. This includes logging usage traffic to our website in the form of server logs. These logs contain information such as your device's Internet Protocol ("IP") address and additional information about your operating system and browser.

For our apps on your phone and other devices, we may utilize third-party tools to record similar information such as your IP address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date that you used our Service, and other statistics.

In both cases listed above, such data is only stored and utilized as long as is needed.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

Our Services does not use these “cookies” explicitly. However, our Services may use third party code and libraries that use “cookies” to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. Please note that if you choose to refuse our cookies, portions of our Service that rely on them may not function properly.
